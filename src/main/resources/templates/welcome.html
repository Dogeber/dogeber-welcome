About Us

WHAT IS Dogeber Coin?

DogeberCoin
It is a utility token with a total amount of 5 billion, which is used for circulation in the Dogeber social financial ecology, and in this social network, people can freely complete the transfer of value.
Dogeber is the world's first Web3-based social network, users can freely reward cryptocurrency to any user, and also freely reward cryptocurrency to the community.
Users replace traditional registration and login with linked wallets.
